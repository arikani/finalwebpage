Our Story

Your Dining Restaurant since 1989 Kathmandu’s newest steakhouse by ari is conveniently located in the centre of Thamel, peacefully tucked away on the first floor of Hotel Trekkers’ Home. Whether you come alone, as a couple or a large group, we look forward to welcoming you in our tastefully decorated restaurant where you can enjoy an excellent steak or a delicious vegetarian meal. Choose table seating or relax on the pillows by our low tables, we even have a selection of board games ready for you to play!
